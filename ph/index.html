Customizable Packages to Fit Your Needs

Every doctor has their own preferred way of working. Some may prefer telehealth shifts for a bit of extra income after the kids are in bed, while others may choose an 8-hour telehealth shift. Some doctors prefer us to provide a vehicle, driver, and all necessary equipment, while others like to supply some or all of it themselves. We offer full flexibility and are happy to provide whatever support you need.

We operate on a private billing system.

We charge a gap fee to ensure you earn more. With a steady stream of patients willing to pay for high-quality telehealth and home visit care, you can take home double or even triple the amount per patient at Bizz Doctor compared to a bulk billing deputising service.

Built-in Healthcare Compliance

While some services still engage in non-compliant healthcare practices, despite the widespread concerns, our app simplifies and streamlines compliance. It enables you to triage your patients, document accurately, and select the correct healthcare items with ease.

You have control over your billings.

Some services bill on behalf of doctors and charge high-rebate urgent items, even when the conditions don't qualify as urgent, putting doctors at risk of healthcare compliance issues. At Bizz Doctor, we give doctors the freedom to bill healthcare as they see fit and charge a gap fee, ensuring there's never a need to bill inappropriately to earn a great income.

Our patients highly appreciate us.

We proudly maintain a 5-Star rating because we provide a world-class service. Our patients are incredibly appreciative, making every shift enjoyable and fulfilling. By joining Bizz Doctor, you’ll become part of a team that is genuinely making a positive impact in the community.

Complete Control Over Your Shift

For each shift, you have full control over whether you choose home visits, telehealth, or both, your start and finish times, how many patients you want to see, and the areas where you'll accept bookings. You can adjust any of these throughout the shift, ensuring you never feel overwhelmed or work later than planned.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to some of the most common questions about our services and how we support healthcare professionals in India.

What types of shifts are available at Bizz Doctor?

At Bizz Doctor, we offer a variety of shifts to suit your needs, including home visits, telehealth consultations, and a combination of both. We have flexible daytime, evening, and overnight shifts available across different regions in India.

How much can I earn working with Bizz Doctor?

Doctors working with us can earn significantly more compared to traditional bulk billing services. For home visits, you can earn up to ₱ 9,400 per visit, and for telehealth consultations, you can earn up to ₱ 5,370 per consultation. This can be double or even triple what you would earn at a bulk billing service.

What kind of support does Bizz Doctor offer during shifts?

When you work with Bizz Doctor, you’re never alone. Our team of highly experienced GP Fellows is just a phone call away, providing clinical support whenever you need it. We also offer assistance with equipment, vehicles, and drivers, based on your preferences.

Is there flexibility in choosing my work schedule?

Yes! You have full control over your schedule. You can choose when you start and finish your shifts, how many patients you'd like to see, and the areas where you’re willing to accept bookings. This flexibility allows you to balance work with personal commitments.

What are the requirements for working with Bizz Doctor?

To work with us, you need to be a registered medical professional with relevant experience in general practice or a specialized field. If you're new to General Practice or looking to return, we offer support and training to help you get started in an after-hours environment.

How does the billing process work?

We operate on a private billing system and charge a gap fee, ensuring you earn more compared to bulk billing services. Our system allows doctors to bill healthcare services as they choose, without any risk of inappropriate billing, ensuring full compliance with healthcare regulations.
